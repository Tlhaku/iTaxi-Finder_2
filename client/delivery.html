“The taxi industry already has the infrastructure and building blocks to facilitate a parcel delivery service, all that remains is a central platform to manage, track and coordinate all order requests and the handovers they require along the way.”

Delivery Extenda

Connect township fleets, depots, and trusted partners to deliver parcels efficiently along existing minibus-taxi corridors. Delivery Extenda wraps bookings, routing, and proof-of-handover into one coordinated workflow.

How it works

  1. Create the order: Customers submit pickup and drop-off points plus an optional retail partner. Orders drop straight onto the live map.
  2. Plan the trip chain: Our routing engine pairs depot hops with taxi routes, scoring combinations via the Google Routes API to minimise total travel distance and time.
  3. Move the parcel: Each handover scans a QR code to confirm custody as parcels travel from collector to rank depots, then to their final reception points.

Live tracking & transparency

  • Map overlays show active pickup and drop-off pins plus the current leg of every parcel.
  • Status timelines highlight completed and pending handovers to keep customers informed.
  • Depot and operator dashboards surface performance metrics, revenue opportunities, and service-level trends.
